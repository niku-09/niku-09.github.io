Applications of LabVIEW for Power Electronics Laboratory

LabVIEW NI-9225 NI-9227 DAQ Assistant Power Electronics Sensor Interfacing
About

Presented a research paper along with Dr. Gunabalan R & team on integrating LabVIEW into undergraduate power electronics lab experiments @ 11th International Mardin Artuklu Scientific Researches Conference, December 2023, TÃ¼rkiye.

The work involved using NI9225 and NI9227 sensors for real-time voltage and current measurement across AC-DC, DC-DC, and AC-AC converters, with DAQ Assistant blocks handling sensor interfacing. Waveforms were monitored live on the LabVIEW front panel, and measured values were validated against theoretical calculations across varying firing angles.

The purpose of this experiment is to encourage the use of LabVIEW interfacing in universities. (AC voltage regulator - example)
